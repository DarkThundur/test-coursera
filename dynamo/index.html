What is a Dynamo?

It is a device which converts mechanical energy into electrical energy.

How does it work?

The dynamo works via using electromagnetic induction.

In electromagnetic induction a magnetic field is moved relative to a conductor thus producing a voltage.

In our dynamo a magnet is rotated around coils of wire generating an AC current.

It generates AC current as the north and south poles are constantly changing relative to the coils.

Where can a dynamo be used

  • In speed bumpers to generate current from moving car wheels
  • In bicycle wheels to power the bicycle lights
  • As a generator to generate electricity for usage
  • Used in handcranked torch lights
  • Used to charge batteries and mobile phones (human powered chargers)
